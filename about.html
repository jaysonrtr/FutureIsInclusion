Our Vision

We envision a world where every child, regardless of ability, is welcomed, celebrated, and fully included in their community. Through our Invitation to Inclusion bags and a growing directory of local inclusive resources, we aim to spark connection, raise awareness, and equip individuals and organizations to create environments where all children can belong and thrive.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote inclusion by equipping caregivers and communities with practical tools, meaningful resources, and local connections. Through the distribution of Invitation to Inclusion bags and a comprehensive directory of inclusive organizations, we empower individuals to advocate for children of all abilities and foster a culture of belonging in every space.
